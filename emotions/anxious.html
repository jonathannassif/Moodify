You’re feeling anxious

Your mind is racing, but you’re not alone. Try these:

Breathe in for 4, hold for 4, out for 4 (Box breathing)
Do a quick body scan — notice tension areas
Journal what you’re anxious about — name it
Do one tiny thing you have control over
Limit caffeine for now
Play calm background sounds or music
“Anxiety does not empty tomorrow of its sorrows, but only empties today of its strength.” — Charles Spurgeon
